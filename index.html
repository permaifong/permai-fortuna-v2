v.Questions

Commonly asked.

01 Do you offer knee pain treatment and knee injection therapy? +
Yes. Our doctors provide intra-articular knee injections and trigger-point injections to relieve joint and muscle pain, alongside assessment and ongoing management of knee and joint conditions.
02 What is an intra-articular (knee) injection? +
It is an injection given directly into the knee joint to reduce inflammation and ease pain. It is a quick, in-clinic procedure performed by our doctors, often used for arthritis and chronic knee pain.
03 Are you really open 24 hours? +
Yes. Permai Polyclinics Fortuna is open 24 hours a day, every day of the year, for both routine and emergency care.
04 Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in? +
Walk-ins are welcome at any time. You can also message us on WhatsApp at 012-939 3537 to arrange a convenient time.
05 Do you offer pre-employment health screening? +
Yes. We provide pre-employment and general health screening packages, including blood pressure, blood glucose, urine tests, and full blood tests.
06 Do you provide emergency treatment and IV drips? +
Yes. We offer emergency treatment around the clock, along with injections and intravenous drips when needed.
07 Where is the clinic located? +
We are at the Fortuna Commercial Centre, Mile 2.5, Jalan Penampang, Kota Kinabalu (Ground Floor, Lot 3, Block D2, Lorong Seroja 2).
